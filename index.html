DESCRIPTION

I am a passionate technologist. I find joy in the act and process of problem solving. Every problem is a process of discovery and learning, from finding vulnerabilities in a system or application, to building software and securing systems. I am driven by exploration and the desire to know how and why systems work the way they do.

I often have strong opinions, but am always open to discussing and challenging assumptions. I value diverse opinions and engaging in respectful, healthy, and constructive discussions about any topic. I fully expect that I will be wrong, a lot, and hope that others will feel comfortable in presenting their view and correction where I am in error.
